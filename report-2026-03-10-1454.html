Synthesis

Three Key Findings

01

Key Finding 1

The convergence of price negotiation and fee transparency as top pain points at a combined 50% reveals that consumer trust is the single biggest barrier to purchase satisfaction in today's automotive retail environment.

02

Key Finding 2

With 45% of buyers open to both new and used vehicles and 46% budgeting between $15K and $34.9K, value optimization rather than brand loyalty is increasingly steering purchase decisions.

03

Key Finding 3

Electrified powertrains now match gasoline in aggregate consumer consideration, suggesting the tipping point for mainstream EV and hybrid adoption is closer than traditional market assumptions indicate.
